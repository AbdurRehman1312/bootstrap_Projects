FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
What is Sky Beings ?

Sky Beings is a forward-looking STEM Education company. We focus on nurturing young minds through exceptional programs in robotics, programming, mechanical design, and 3D printing, encouraging them to excel in these vital fields.

Who can enroll with Sky Beings?

Sky Beings welcomes students from all backgrounds to enroll in our STEM education programs. Regardless of their previous experience or field of interest, we provide a supportive and inclusive learning environment for everyone to explore and excel in STEM disciplines.
