About Redbox

Red Box smart lockers for receiving internet orders - Red Box provides the service of receiving and retrieving parcels through Red Box smart lockers. Where you can receive packages from different areas of the city, 24 hours a day.

Services and prices

Delivery

  • 12 Saudi riyals / 15 kg - for shipments between/within cities
  • 2 SAR/kg additional

Return

If the shipment has been deposited, it will be returned free of charge, taking into account the cost of delivering the shipment

  • If the shipment is not deposited, it will be returned for free

Return

  • 10 Saudi riyals - for orders less than 5 shipments

Cash on delivery

  • 0.8% made usage fee
  • 2.5% Visa usage fee

* Prices do not include value added tax
